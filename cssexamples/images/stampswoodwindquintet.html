What is the Stamps Woodwind Quintet?

The Stamps Woodwind Quintet is a chamber music group composed of 5 undergraduate music performance majors, Lia Suk, Kareema Colson, Eamon Comiskey, Haley Avery, and Clark Walker. These students were chosen from a rigorous audition process that took place before their first year at Frost. They will spend all 4 years of their undergraduate degree together, growing, and performing.

Awards and Recognitions

The Stamps Woodwind Quintet have been semi-finalists in the 2025 Coltman Chamber Music Competition, played Aires Tropicales with composer Paquito D’Rivera, Received coachings and played in concerts with Time For Three, as well as played in out-reach concerts at The Palace, Winter Wonderful, and Frost Welcome Events.
